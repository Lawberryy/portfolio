About me

Hello! My name is Sixtine, I'm a French sophomore student in coding & digital innovation at IIM Digital School in Paris. I've been interested in web development for more than a year now and I aspire to become a full-stack web developer by the end of my studies. For now, I'm more comfortable with frontend langages.

Apart from my studies in coding, I have several hobbies as well. I recently started taking interest in learning the korean & japanese langages. Their music, animation and film culture is what pushed me in that direction. I also like traveling, playing video games and reading on my free time.
